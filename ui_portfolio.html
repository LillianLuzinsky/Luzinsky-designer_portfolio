Motion Graphics

Adobe Illlustrator   •   Adobe After Effects   •   Adobe InDesign

10xEvolve needed an explainer video for their startup. Although I had no previous experience with video editing software or animation, I was given the opportunity to learn how to create this video. I chose to use typography to tell a story and then selected the right illustrations to convey the message.

I combined them with Adobe InDesign and created an animated GIF with the InDesign document. Once the storyboard design was approved, I started building the animation in Adobe After Effects. I really enjoyed producing this animation, as it allowed me to learn a new program, become a better developer and designer, and break down compositions into smaller components.
